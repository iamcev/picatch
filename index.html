About Us

Picatch is an app dedicated to bring you happiness through a game of exciting, worldwide geocaching. Try making a post. Hide a cache anywhere (but not in private property, hazardous locations, etc.) and give us your name, the name of your post, a broad location, and a short description. Have fun Picatching!!
